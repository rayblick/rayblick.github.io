Virtual Ecology

Optimize ecological surveys



The VE toolset can optimize transect length in an ecological monitoring program. The primary goal is to reduce transect length (and overall cost) without losing the power to detect a difference between surveys (e.g. seasonal surveys).

The virtual ecologist acts as a second observer calibrated by your staff.

These tools assume that the data were collected from transects (which are variable in length), and each transect contains sequential data collection points. E.g. A 50 meter transect with 1m2 plots spaced 4 meters apart. These tools also assume that you are assessing grouped data (e.g. functional groups, lifeforms, guilds or a custom list of groupings used to cluster species) at an individual location.
